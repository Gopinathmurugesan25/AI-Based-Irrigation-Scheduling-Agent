The Problem

Why Traditional Irrigation Fails

Farmers across the globe lose crops and water to outdated irrigation methods. The cost is enormous — for them and for the planet.

Water Wastage

Over-irrigation wastes up to 70% of fresh water used in agriculture, depleting groundwater tables and increasing costs.

Crop Yield Loss

Under-irrigation stresses crops at critical growth stages, reducing harvest yield by 20–40% and threatening food security.

Unpredictable Weather

Climate variability makes manual irrigation scheduling unreliable. Farmers relying on intuition often irrigate just before rain.

No Real-Time Data

Without live soil and weather data, decisions are made blindly — leading to inefficient water use and unnecessary expenses.

Features

Everything You Need

Six core capabilities that make AgroMind the most intelligent irrigation assistant available for modern farming.

Real-Time Soil Monitoring

Continuous readings from IoT sensors track soil moisture, temperature, and EC levels — updated every 15 minutes for live insight.

Weather-Aware Scheduling

Integrates 72-hour weather forecasts to prevent redundant irrigation before rain — saving water and reducing energy costs.

Crop-Specific Water Needs

Each crop profile contains growth-stage water requirements. The AI adapts its recommendations to your specific crop and season.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Every recommendation comes with a plain-language explanation — “Why should I skip irrigation today?” is always answered.

Mobile Dashboard

Farmers access recommendations, live sensor data, and field history from a clean, simple mobile-first dashboard — no tech expertise needed.

Usage History & Logs

Full audit trail of every irrigation event — including AI rationale, sensor state, and outcomes — for post-season analysis and reporting.

Good for Farmers. Great for the Planet.
